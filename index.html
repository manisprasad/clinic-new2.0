ABOUT Dr.Alka Sharma
Dr. Alka Sharma

B.Sc., B.H.M.S, CSD, CCAH

Ex. Head Medical Services, Dr. Batras' Positive Health Clinic Private Limited

Dr. Alka Sharma holds a Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine and Surgery. She is also certified in CSD (Skin Disease) and CCAH (Advanced Homeopathy) from MUHS, Nashik. With seven years of experience, she specializes in treating acute and chronic ailments, including Syndrome, skin diseases, piles, Musculoskeletal disorders, children's diseases, digestive problems, genitourinary disorders, gynecological disorders, allergic bronchitis, asthma, arthritis, migraine, hair problems, and irritable bowel, among others.
