Allergies

Allergies are an overreaction of the immune system to substances that are generally harmless. Symptoms can include sneezing, itching, and rashes.

Homeopathic Treatment for Allergies

Homeopathy treats allergies by addressing the underlying causes and strengthening the immune system. Common remedies include:

  • Allium Cepa - For sneezing and runny nose.
  • Apis Mellifica - For swelling and redness.
  • Arsenicum Album - For burning sensations and anxiety.
